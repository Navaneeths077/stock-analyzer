📸 How Image Import Works

Step 1: Upload a screenshot of stock metrics (table, webpage, etc.)
Step 2: AI extracts text using OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
Step 3: Numbers are matched to financial metrics automatically
Step 4: Review extracted values and import to form
Step 5: Click Analyze for instant results

Works with: NSE/BSE apps, Yahoo Finance, Excel spreadsheets, PDFs (screenshots), financial websites

📊 Supported Metrics

Valuation: P/E, Book Value, Dividend, EPS

Profitability: ROE, ROCE, Margins

Growth: Revenue, Profit, CAGR

Health: D/E, Liquidity, Reserves, Ownership

⚠️ Tips for Better Accuracy

  • Use high-resolution images
  • Ensure good lighting (avoid shadows)
  • Avoid skewed/rotated images
  • Include metric names alongside values
  • Separate numbers with spaces for clarity
  • Works best with printed/digital text (not handwritten)
